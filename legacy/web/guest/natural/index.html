Natural Resource & Environmental protecton Affairs Standing Committee


 

Duty and Responsibilities

The Committee has the following Duties and Responsibilities

 

1.      Supervises the effective utilization and development of water resource and mineral. And also, the expansion of hydropower stations and electricity provision in the country.
2.       makes sure whether assistance is given for the increasing demand of pure drinking water supply; for the development of   alternative energy sources  t and expansion of  middle and higher irrigation scheme and survey studies.
3.       follows up whether the National metrology Agency is duly releasing reliable and timely weather and climate forecast information to the pertinent bodies so that the country’s natural resources well preserved, environmental protection sustains and domestic and international flight will be safe and cost effective as well.
4.      checks whether a conducive condition is created for business men to invest on mineral extractions and ensures if the mining investment is run in compatible with the contract agreement concluded.
5.      makes sure that policies rules, strategies and plans of supervisee institutions duly implemented .
6.      oversights whether the executive bodies that are under the jurisdiction of the committee are properly discharging the duties granted under law, and the proper utilization of their budget.  Also, whether cross sectoral activities have been carried out there in.

Vision

House envisions being an institution of exemplary and developed multiparty parliamentary democracy in Africa by 2023

Mission

  • Legislating laws that contribute to the democratic system building, ensuring of good governance and peace, and the socio-economic development;

  • Following up and supervising the proper implementation of the laws, government polices and plans, and effective utilization of the resources allocated for executing the plans and policies thereby ensure the public is beneficial.

  • Organizing a procedure and a system that helps to consolidate the multiparty parliamentary democracy and ensure Just full and fair participation in the activities of the House.
