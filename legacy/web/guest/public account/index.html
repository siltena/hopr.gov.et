Public Accounts Affairs Standing Committee

 

Duty and Responsibilities

1.      supervises the proper utilization of Federal Government budget.
2.      investigates the Federal Auditor General  report whether it focuses on the following whilst auditing:
A.    how the allocated budget is used targeting the planned program and service,
B.      the expenditures approved by  competent  body,
C.      budget transfer  made in conformity with the financial law.
3.   evaluates audit reports conducted by the Auditor General as ordered by the House.
4.   scrutinizes compelling reasons for the additional budget request of any government bodies other than the amount allocated in the fiscal year, also, responsible for  presenting decision proposal upon the additional budget request to the House accordingly .
5.  scrutinizes whether policies, rules, strategies, programs and plans of offices supervised by the standing committee properly implemented.
6.      whether executive bodies accountable to this standing committee are duly exercising their  power and carrying out their duties as well; also, the effective   utilization of their budget and whether they give due attention to cross sectoral issues.

Vision

House envisions being an institution of exemplary and developed multiparty parliamentary democracy in Africa by 2023

Mission

  • Legislating laws that contribute to the democratic system building, ensuring of good governance and peace, and the socio-economic development;

  • Following up and supervising the proper implementation of the laws, government polices and plans, and effective utilization of the resources allocated for executing the plans and policies thereby ensure the public is beneficial.

  • Organizing a procedure and a system that helps to consolidate the multiparty parliamentary democracy and ensure Just full and fair participation in the activities of the House.
