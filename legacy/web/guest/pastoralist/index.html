Pastoralists' Affairs Standing Committee

 

Duty and Responsibilities

The Pastoralists Affairs Standing Committee has the following major duties;
 

1.      makes sure  the constitutional rights of the pastoralists are protected.

2.  followes up and supervises whether government has provided special support to pastoralists.

3. oversights the Federal Government endeavors to bring a rapid development in pastoralist areas to ensure food security and    social progress.

4.  scrutinizes whether the  Federal Government considers  the existing  situations of pastoralist areas whilst adopting national wide rules, policies, programes and plans.

5.    follows up and supervises the expansion of capacity building institutions, especially boarding  and mobile schools as well as other day-schools in order to build the pastoralists capacity as quickly as possible .

6.  evaluates the proper utilization of  the government budget in the pastoralists areas  in order that the  the pastoralists could secure  health service, market infrastructure, fodder and water for their livelihood, livestock.

7.    makes sure the villagization programme planned in the pastoralists area are implemented in a manner accounted the willingness of the pastoralists in that the availability of adequate infrastructures.

8.      appriase whether extension assistance is provided to the pastoralist.

9. checks whether pre-warning studies are being implemented in the pastoralist areas.

10. whether researches gearing to boost pastoralist’s productivity, also the benefit  they gain from  other agricultural products.

11. Oversights whther a suitable condition is put in place to stop harmful traditional practices in the pastoralist areas.

12. checkes due attention as well as a coordinated support is given to regions which need special assistance.

13. scrutinizes whether policies, rules, strategies, programs and plans of      offices supervised by the standing committee properly        implemented. appraises whether executive bodies accountable to this  standing  committee are duly exercising their  power and carrying out their duties as well; also the effective   utilization of their budget and whether they give due attention to cross sectoral issues.

Vision
