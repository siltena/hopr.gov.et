Figures and Facts

Climate


There are two seasons: the dry season prevails from October through May; the wet season runs from June to September.

Economic Indicators


About 90 per cent of the population earn their living from the land, mainly as subsistence farmers. Agriculture is the backbone of the national economy and the principal exports from this sector are coffee, oil seeds, and pulses, flowers, vegetables, sugar and foodstuffs for animals. There is also a thriving livestock sector exporting cattle and the hoof and hides and skins.

Transport and Communication


Roads - There are Some 8,295 kilometers of asphalt roads with a further 44,848 kilometers of gravel and dry- weather roads
Air - Ethiopian Airlines has an extensive domestic and International network flight with 17 and 66 destinations respectively.

Currency Unit


The local currency is the Ethiopian Birr, made up of 100 cents. Foreign currency may only be changed at authorized banks and hotels.

Vision

House envisions being an institution of exemplary and developed multiparty parliamentary democracy in Africa by 2023

Mission

  • Legislating laws that contribute to the democratic system building, ensuring of good governance and peace, and the socio-economic development;

  • Following up and supervising the proper implementation of the laws, government polices and plans, and effective utilization of the resources allocated for executing the plans and policies thereby ensure the public is beneficial.

  • Organizing a procedure and a system that helps to consolidate the multiparty parliamentary democracy and ensure Just full and fair participation in the activities of the House.
