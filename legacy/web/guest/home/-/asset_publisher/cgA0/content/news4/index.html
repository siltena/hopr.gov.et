Honor Aba Dula Gemeda

Speaker's message The House of peoples representatives, in accordance with article 54(1) of the constitution of the FDRE, is the highest authority of the Federal Government established by representatives elected for a term of 5 years on the basis of universal suffrage and by direct, free and fair elections held by secret ballot. The House has the power of legislating, calling and questioning the prime Minister and other Federal officials and investigating the executive's conduct and discharge of its responsibilities. The House has also major powers of approving the appointment of the Federal Judges, Ministers, commissioners, the Federal Auditor General and other officials whose appointment is required by law to be approved by it. Parliament appoints Dr. Mulatu Teshome president of FDRE for the next 6 years

     

    The 4th House of Peoples’ Representatives and the House of the Federation of the FDRE, 4th year tenure, the first joint regular sitting held on 07 October 2013. 
    The session opened by the former FDRE president H.E Ato Girma Woldegiorgis farewell speech.  H.E Ato Girma Woldegiorgis   expressed his heartfelt wishing, all the best for the incoming president, and handed over the presidency entrusted to him for the last two terms of office.
    The incoming president, H.E Dr. Mulatu Teshome was sworn the oath    through federal Supreme Court president, Ato Tegene Getaneh to faithfully carry out the high responsibility entrusted to him.  
     In his speech to the two Houses, H.E, President Dr. Mulatu Teshome assured that he contributes his fair share to the country’s all-rounded development and pledged to diligently and loyally discharge the responsibilities placed on him.

Vision

House envisions being an institution of exemplary and developed multiparty parliamentary democracy in Africa by 2023

Mission

  • Legislating laws that contribute to the democratic system building, ensuring of good governance and peace, and the socio-economic development;

  • Following up and supervising the proper implementation of the laws, government polices and plans, and effective utilization of the resources allocated for executing the plans and policies thereby ensure the public is beneficial.

  • Organizing a procedure and a system that helps to consolidate the multiparty parliamentary democracy and ensure Just full and fair participation in the activities of the House.
